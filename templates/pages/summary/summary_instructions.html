Summary of public transportation reporting

Please take a moment to read the instructions.
  • The first step of the reporting process is to review and update your organization’s coversheet information, which provides some basic information about your organization and service area.
  • After you press the ‘let’s get started’ button below, you will be taken to the first page with your organization’s information as it appeared in last year’s summary.
  • If you need to make any edits, please do so.
  • Once you are satisfied with the content press the 'update or confirm' button, to be taken to the next screen.
  • When you have completed every section you will be asked if you would like to submit your coversheet for review.
  • You may review what your information prior to submitting it, using the tabs above.
  • It is recommended that you complete each page in order.

We are here to help! If at any point you get stuck or have difficulties please reach out to us and ask for help.

Up to date contact information is available on the help page.
